WHAT IS IT?

Nam Lhai is a traditional Thai weaving technique that creates flowing patterns reminiscent of water streams, inspired by the Nan rivers of the Chao Praya River.

ORIGIN & SIGNIFICANCE

The Nan River runs through the northern part of Thailand. It is a tributary of the Chao Phraya River, which is one of the major rivers in Thailand. responsible for many things - water supply, biodiversity, cultural and historical significance for local folklore, songs, festivals etc.
